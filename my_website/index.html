About Me

My Background

Hey! For nearly a decade, I have been in academia, studying philosophy. I'm currently finishing up a PdD in philosophy at University of Virginia. I spend a lot of time writing about complicated problems. I also teach. I'm usually a teaching assistant, which involves teaching small sections weekly and meeting with students one on one. But I've also developed a course from scratch and taught it. That is one of my proudest accomplishments! I've been a TA for logic a few times, and have always enjoyed it. That's what drew me to software engineering. Logic is great, and I want to keep using it!
